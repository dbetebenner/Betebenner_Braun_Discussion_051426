# Betebenner-Braun Discussion · May 14, 2026

This repository hosts a RevealJS presentation prepared for discussion between Damian W. Betebenner and Henry I. Braun on **longitudinal inference without longitudinal data**, with emphasis on the measurement-theory questions raised by copula-based extensions of TAMP.

## Focus

The presentation responds to Henry Braun's May 2026 comments and questions, especially:

- the distinction between mean SGPc and median SGPc for decision making;
- admissible transformations, ordinal meaning, and measurement invariance;
- the difference between interval-scale and vertical-scale claims;
- TAMP as a deterministic, rank-preserving boundary case;
- TAMPc as a stochastic copula-based extension using fitted growth regimes.
